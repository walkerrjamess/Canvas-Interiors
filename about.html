About Canvas Interiors

Crafting extraordinary spaces through vision, precision, and artistry

Studio Overview

Founded in the heart of London, Canvas Interiors represents the pinnacle of contemporary interior design. We specialise in creating bespoke residential and commercial spaces that seamlessly blend functionality with aesthetic excellence. Our approach combines traditional British craftsmanship with cutting-edge visualisation technology, ensuring every project exceeds our clients' expectations.

At Canvas Interiors, we believe that exceptional design begins with understanding. Each project starts with an in-depth consultation where we explore not just what our clients want, but how they live, work, and dream. This foundation allows us to create spaces that are not merely beautiful, but truly transformative.

Our commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing ensures that every material we specify not only meets our exacting standards for beauty and quality, but also aligns with our values of environmental responsibility. From reclaimed hardwoods to low-impact textiles, we curate materials that tell a story of conscious luxury.

Principal Designer

Leading Canvas Interiors is our Principal Designer, whose vision has shaped London's most prestigious residential and commercial spaces over the past fifteen years. With a background in architecture from the Royal College of Art and extensive experience working with heritage properties, our approach balances respect for historical context with bold contemporary vision. This unique perspective has made Canvas Interiors the preferred choice for discerning clients seeking both innovation and timeless elegance.

Meet the Team

Our multidisciplinary design team brings together expertise in interior architecture, spatial planning, and decorative arts. Each designer specialises in specific typologies, from heritage residential projects to cutting-edge commercial spaces, ensuring every client benefits from deep sector knowledge.

Our award-winning visualisation specialists combine technical expertise with artistic vision to create photo-realistic renderings that accurately represent the final space. Using the latest in CGI technology and lighting simulation, they bring designs to life before construction begins.

Our project management team ensures seamless coordination between all stakeholders, from initial concept through to final installation. With backgrounds in both design and construction, they bridge the gap between creative vision and practical execution.
